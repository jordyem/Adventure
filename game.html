ABOUT THE GAME

In general, the idea we have is to develop a 2D platform game, in this case the objective will be to get the player to the flag, which will be the goal, but in the course of the road there will be obstacles that will prevent it, so the player must try to avoid them and reach the end. To make this game you will be using a base tutorial, in this case things like the items, the environment and the player will be modified. We will try to cover and apply everything seen during the course.
